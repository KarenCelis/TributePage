Toto la Momposina

Toto La Momposina's entire life has been dedicated to representing the music of the Caribbean coast of Colombia.

Here's a time line of Toto la Momposina:

  • 1950 - When she was a child, the conflict of La Violencia forced her family to flee their home and move to the capital, Bogotá. There, her mother started a dance group from which Toto emerged as a promising singer.
  • 1960- Forming her own grouping
  • 1970- Began performing outside of Colombia, touring Latin America, Eastern and Western Europe, and the United States.
  • 1982She accompanied Gabriel García Márquez to Stockholm as part of the Colombian delegation to perform at the Nobel Prize ceremony.
  • 1990- Her world fame was cemented by a WOMAD tour on three continents, the launch of La Candela Viva with Real World Records and the many international shows and recordings that followed.
  • 1996- Album Carmelina
  • 1999- Album Pacantó with MTM Colombia.
  • 2006- Was awarded the WOMEX Lifetime Achievement Award
  • 2013- Latin Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award. His legacy lives on and the latest album is ‘Tamborero’, a recreation of the classic ‘La Candela Viva’, with Real World Records

Toto continues to work tirelessly to promote the music of his homeland, driven by passion and the simple joy of doing concerts.
